About Robson

A legacy of warmth and sophistication.

Where stories begin

Since 1924, Robson has been a sanctuary for travellers seeking the extraordinary. Our heritage blends with contemporary design, offering 120 bespoke rooms, a world-class spa, and a culinary journey that celebrates local flavours.

Every detail — from the hand-picked art to the organic linen — is curated to create an atmosphere of effortless luxury.
